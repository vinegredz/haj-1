Inhumanity at the Moscow Consulate

Moscow capital of Russia and home to 300,000 Jews. Jewish applicants for Israeli citizenship sometimes travel for hours by train and bus from more remote cities to get to their appointment. Once there, they are treated like cattle in unbelievable conditions. Read our list of grievances against the Moscow consulate in particular.

Opaque Requirements

Israeli consulates in Russia and the former Soviet Union require an exhaustive list of documentation from an applicant for repatriation to Israel. It's not enough to have merely a letter from a Rabbi, as it is in the west. Read on to be astounded by the sheer volume and obscurity of the requirements.
